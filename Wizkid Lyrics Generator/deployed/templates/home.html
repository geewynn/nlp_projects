Wizkid Song Lyrics Prediction.

Wizkid Song Lyrics Predictor is a fun project built by training a LSTM model on 50 of Wizkids songs.

WizKid(Ayodeji Ibrahim Balogun) is a Nigerian Superstar Musician. He has released various hit music since he signed for Empiree Mates Entertainment (E.M.E).
He is known both in the local and International scenes, he has partnered with various artists and has won various awards
His music genre are Afrobeats, reggae, dancehall, hip-hop and pop.
